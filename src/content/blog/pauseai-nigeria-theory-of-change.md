## Mission

PauseAI Nigeria seeks to contribute to a global halt on frontier AI development until it can be made safe and placed under meaningful democratic control, by mobilizing public awareness, policy engagement, and coordinated civic action within Nigeria.

## What We Want

A world in which superhuman AI systems are not developed or deployed until:

- Robust safety guarantees exist.
- Democratic institutions have effective oversight and control.

This pause must be international, not local, due to global race dynamics in AI development.

## The Problem We Are Addressing (Nigeria Context)

Despite growing global concern about AI risks, four barriers remain:

1. Low policy awareness in Nigeria.

- AI existential and systemic risks are largely absent from policy conversations.
- Policymakers often prioritize short-term innovation and economic upside over long-term safety.

2. Global race dynamics.

- Countries fear being left behind economically and strategically.
- Even informed actors are incentivized to prioritize speed over safety.
- Nigeria risks becoming a passive rule-taker rather than an informed voice in global AI governance.

3. Lack of urgency.

- AI progress is consistently underestimated.
- Rapid capability advances have not yet translated into proportional urgency in Nigerian discourse.

4. Psychological and Overton-window constraints.

- Existential AI risk is still perceived as too extreme or speculative.
- Serious pause discussion remains taboo in many political and elite spaces.
- Public concern exists but is under-organized and under-articulated.

## Our Core Assumption

If Nigerian policymakers, media, students, and civil society understand:

- the scale of AI risks,
- Nigeria's stake in global AI governance,
- and the feasibility of coordinated international action,

then Nigeria can:

- support global pause initiatives,
- influence international negotiations,
- and avoid being sidelined in decisions that affect humanity's future.

## How Change Happens

### Short-Term Outcomes

- Increased public awareness of AI safety risks in Nigeria.
- Nigerian policymakers exposed to AI pause arguments.
- A visible, credible PauseAI Nigeria chapter.

### Medium-Term Outcomes

- AI risk enters Nigerian policy and media discourse.
- Nigerian representatives engage international AI governance discussions.
- Nigeria supports or aligns with international pause initiatives.

### Long-Term Impact

- Contribution to international momentum toward:
  - an AI pause treaty, or
  - supply-chain-based halts on frontier AI development.
- Reduced risk of catastrophic or existential harm from uncontrolled AI.

## Our Core Strategies

### 1. Grow the Movement

- Build a national volunteer base through online organizing, student engagement, and accessible messaging.
- Empower volunteers to take concrete action with minimal barriers.

### 2. Inform and Shape Public Discourse

- Publish educational content (articles, explainers, social content).
- Participate in media (radio, TV, podcasts, online platforms).
- Host public events and discussions.
- Partner with influencers, academics, and civil society organizations.

### 3. Lobbying and Policy Engagement

- Engage policymakers, advisers, and regulators through email campaigns, meetings, and policy briefs.
- Encourage Nigerian leaders to:
  - recognize AI existential risks,
  - support international AI pause discussions,
  - and participate responsibly in global AI summits and treaty processes.

### 4. Protests and Visible Civic Action

- Organize peaceful, high-visibility actions adapted to Nigerian realities.
- Use virtual protests where logistics or security constraints are high.
- Coordinate with global protest moments.
- Use civic action to shift the Overton window, attract media attention, and recruit volunteers.

### 5. Youth and University Engagement

- Host AI safety debates in Nigerian universities.
- Engage students as future policymakers, technologists, and opinion leaders.
- Build a long-term pipeline of informed AI safety advocates.

## What We Do Not Do

- We do not promote or tolerate violence.
- We do not take sides in unrelated political, religious, or ideological issues.
- We do not misrepresent facts or exaggerate claims.

PauseAI Nigeria is committed to peaceful, honest, evidence-based advocacy aligned with PauseAI Global principles.

## Nigeria-Specific Priority Actions

Given local conditions, PauseAI Nigeria emphasizes:

- lobbying and policy engagement,
- strategic media outreach,
- virtual protests,
- and university debates on AI safety.

## Conclusion

PauseAI Nigeria exists to ensure that Nigeria is not silent, unprepared, or sidelined in decisions about humanity's AI future. By mobilizing public awareness, influencing policy discourse, and aligning with global PauseAI efforts, we aim to play a meaningful role in securing a safer future for all.
